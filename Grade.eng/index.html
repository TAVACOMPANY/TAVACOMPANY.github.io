11 grade

            
So 11 years have passed.
It was the turn of our class to enter adulthood.
All this time, the teachers gave us their affection,
kindness, and knowledge.
We are very grateful to them for this.
They brought us up, made us stronger,
and helped us to overcome difficulties.
And we admire that they, despite everything,
they courageously performed their duty.
School has given us more than just knowledge.
It gave an irreplaceable life experience.
It prepared us for a new interesting life,
as a caring and kind mother, without demanding anything in return.
We spent the best years of our life at school – our youth.
Here we have discovered our inner world, known our inner Self.
School is our second home,
and every time we will come here with a smile and a good mood.
